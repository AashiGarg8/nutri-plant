Advantages of Fruit Plants in the Environment


Biodiversity:

  • Support various species, including pollinators and wildlife.

Soil Enrichment:

  • Improve soil quality through leaf litter and root systems.

Carbon Sequestration:

  • Help reduce carbon levels in the atmosphere.

Food Source:

  • Provide nutrition for animals and humans.

Erosion Control:

  • Stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Common Diseases and Cures

Apple Trees

  • Apple Scab: Use fungicides; remove fallen leaves.
  • Fire Blight: Prune infected branches; apply copper sprays.

Citrus Trees

  • Citrus Canker: Apply bactericides; remove infected fruit.
  • Greening Disease: No cure; remove and destroy infected trees.

Grapevines

  • Powdery Mildew: Use sulfur-based fungicides; ensure good air circulation.
  • Downy Mildew: Apply copper-based fungicides; avoid overhead watering.
