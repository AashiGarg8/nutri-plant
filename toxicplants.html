Advantages of Toxic Plants in the Environment


Biodiversity:

  • Support diverse ecosystems.

Pest Control:

  • Naturally repel pests.

Medicinal Uses:

  • Source of important drugs.

Soil Health:

  • Prevent overgrazing and maintain balance.

Ornamental Value:

  • Aesthetic use in landscaping.

Common Diseases and Cures

Oleander

  • Oleander Leaf Scorch: No cure; control insect vectors.
  • Fungal Infections: Use fungicides; remove infected leaves.

Foxglove

  • Powdery Mildew: Apply fungicides; improve air circulation.
  • Crown Rot: Improve drainage; avoid overwatering.

Nightshade

  • Early Blight: Use fungicides; practice crop rotation.
  • Verticillium Wilt: No cure; plant resistant varieties.
